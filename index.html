About Me

Programmer, Researcher, and Student

I am a second-year MS student at the Electrical Engineering And Computer Science Department at Pennsylvania State University. I am working under the supervision of Professor Bhuvan Urgaonkar and Professor Viveck Cadambe.

I have been working on cost-efficient geo-distributed storage systems leveraging both replication and erasure coding. My colleagues and I developed an optimization framework to pick the near-optimal configuration for deploying a linearizable key-value store. We also created a linearizable key-value store using ABD and CAS that periodically calls our optimizer to find the optimal configuration and change the objects' configuration to the optimal one while maintaining linearizability.
(Currently, the project is private due to the anonymity-related policy of the conference we have sent our paper to.)

Currently, I am doing a Summer internship at Yugabyte. My responsibility is to design and implement methods to resize the VMs in a cluster that runs YugabyteDB without interruption to the clients' operations. It has been a great experience, and so far, I could be exposed to and learn tools such as Ansible and python cloud libraries for AWS (Boto3) and GCP. These tools are essential to tackle real-world cloud computing problems.

I am looking for full-time positions in related areas to start a career in January 2022. Please have a glance at my CV and feel free to contact me.
